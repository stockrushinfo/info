Delete your account

How to permanently delete your StockRush account and all associated data.

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Steps to delete your account

1
Open the menu
Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner of the app to open the drawer.
2
Go to Settings
In the drawer, tap Settings to open the settings screen.
3
Tap "Delete account"
Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and tap Delete account. A confirmation dialog will appear.
4
Confirm deletion
Tap Delete permanently to confirm. Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Data Location Action
User account (Google ID, email, name, refresh token) Server Deleted immediately
Portfolio (tickers, quantities, amounts) Device Deleted immediately
Settings (currency, benchmark, preferences) Device Deleted immediately
Authentication tokens Device Deleted immediately
Widget cached data Device Deleted immediately
Uploaded broker files Server Never stored

Data retention

StockRush does not retain any user data after account deletion. No backups, logs, or anonymized copies of your data are kept. The deletion is immediate and permanent.

Alternative: request deletion via email

If you can no longer access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to stockrush.app@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Google account. We will process your request within 3 business days.

Privacy Policy

Last updated:

StockRush ("we", "us", or "our") operates the StockRush mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Authentication data

We use Google Sign-In to authenticate users. When you sign in, we receive your Google account email address and a unique identifier. This data is used solely to create and manage your account within StockRush.

1.2 Data stored on our server

We store the minimum data necessary for authentication and account management:

  • Google account identifier
  • Email address (used to identify your account for support and deletion requests)
  • Display name (from your Google profile)
  • Refresh token (to keep you logged in)

We do not store your portfolio data, financial information, or any other personal data on our servers.

1.3 Broker file upload

You may import your portfolio by uploading a broker statement file (Excel/CSV). The file is sent to our server solely to enable the parsing functionality of the app. We extract portfolio-related information such as ticker symbols, quantities, and transaction amounts, and return the structured data to your device.

The uploaded file is processed temporarily in memory and is not stored after processing. We do not retain, store, or intentionally log the contents of the file on our servers.

1.4 Data stored on your device

Your portfolio data (tickers, quantities, amounts, settings, benchmarks) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device except as described in Sections 1.3 and 3.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad tracking
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties

3. Third-party services

The App communicates with our backend server to:

  • Authenticate users via Google Sign-In
  • Fetch real-time market data (stock prices, exchange rates)
  • Process uploaded broker files for portfolio import

We rely on third-party services to operate the App, including:

  • Google (for authentication and crash reporting via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Financial data providers (e.g. Twelve Data, Massive API) for market data

These services may receive limited technical data necessary to process requests (such as ticker symbols or standard network information). We do not send personal portfolio data or user-identifying information to these providers.

Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled by Google Sign-In. We receive a user identifier to enable account creation and authentication within the App. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google processes your data.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports in order to improve app stability and fix bugs. This data may include device model, operating system version, app state, and stack traces.

Crash reports are not designed to include directly identifying personal data, portfolio information, or financial data.

For more information, see Firebase Privacy.

Analytics

We use analytics services such as Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its performance and features.

This may include app usage events, screen views, session duration, device information, and app performance metrics.

This data is used in aggregated form and is not used to identify individual users.

4. Widget

The StockRush widget displays your portfolio performance on your home screen. The widget reads data stored locally on your device and periodically fetches updated market prices from our server. Only ticker symbols and your preferred currency are sent to the server — no personal or financial data.

5. Data security

All communication between the App and our server is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. We do not have access to your portfolio data.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Settings → Delete account) or by contacting us at stockrush.app@gmail.com.

Account deletion permanently removes all server-side data associated with your account, including your account identifier, email address, display name, and authentication tokens.

Local data stored on your device can be removed by using the in-app deletion feature or by uninstalling the application.

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion.

7. Children's privacy

StockRush is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
